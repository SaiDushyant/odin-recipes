Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter or margarine
  • ½ cup milk

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly.
  4. Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon.
  5. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup.
  7. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
